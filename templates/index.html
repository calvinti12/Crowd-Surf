About CrowdSurf

CrowdSurf is an intuitive user-based concert tracking platform. Users add new concerts to track and Crowdsurf automatically gathers data on and graphs their ticket prices and outstanding ticket volumes.

CrowdSurf updates its database dynamically in real-time, tracks 'fluxes' - sudden changes in volumes and pricing as providers reprice and sell out, as well as all-time-highs and -lows. The strength in the program comes from its scalability - every user contributes each time they add a new concert to Crowdsurf as they then become available for all other users - and so CrowdSurf increases in power as our user-base grows.

Designed by Sean Hughes and Thushan Puhalendran, Harvard College, 2016.

Features

Search and add any concert around the world

View all currently tracked concerts by all users

Receive up-to-date ticket price changes

Graph historical data on ticket prices and volumes dynamically

Check past highs, lows, volumes and flux
